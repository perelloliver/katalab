# Job Description: Senior Backend Engineer (Core Systems)

**Location**: Remote / Hybrid (London)
**Team**: Core Ledger Team

## The Role
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join the Core Ledger team. You will be responsible for designing and building the next generation of our ledger service, which needs to handle the massive scale of our growing user base. You will deal with critical financial data where eventual consistency is not an option.

## What You'll Do
- Architect and build scalable microservices in Go.
- Migrate legacy Node.js monolith components to distributed Go services.
- Design high-throughput, low-latency transaction processing systems.
- Optimize database queries and schema designs for PostgreSQL and DynamoDB.
- Mentor junior engineers and drive technical excellence in the team.
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response.

## Requirements
- **Experience**: 5+ years of backend engineering experience.
- **Language**: Strong proficiency in Go or Java/C++. Willingness to work primarily in Go.
- **Systems**: Deep understanding of distributed systems patterns (idempotency, distributed transactions, SAGAs).
- **Databases**: Expert knowledge of SQL (PostgreSQL) and NoSQL stores.
- **Cloud Native**: Solid experience with Kubernetes, Docker, and AWS services.
- **Quality**: Passion for TDD, clean code, and automated testing.

## Nice to Haves
- Experience with Event Sourcing / CQRS.
- Financial services or payments background.
- Experience with Service Mesh (Istio/Linkerd).
- Open source contributions.
